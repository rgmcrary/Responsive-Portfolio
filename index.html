About Me

I have always been too analytic for my own good, so naturally, coding seemed to be a great fit. I enjoy creating and disassembling things to see how they "work" and I think this is a perfect avenue to explore and take advantage of this natural tendency.

My hobbies include cooking, music (both recorded and live), video games and traveling. I have studied the Korean language and enjoy cooking Korean cuisine as well as any cuisine. I like to look at menus and recreate what I ordered at home. My music taste ranges greatly and I listen to music every chance I can get as well as go to concerts, both locally and around the country. I currently have over 200 video games and growing, although my time doesn't seem to be growing with the collection size.
